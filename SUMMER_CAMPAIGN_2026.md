## 4. 🔄 Conversion Funnel
*How to turn a $300 pressure wash into a $1,440/year mowing client.*

### The Flow

```
High-ticket job completed (PW, cleanup, mulch)
  → On-site upsell conversation (same day)
    → Follow-up text (Day 3)
      → Follow-up text (Day 10)
        → Recurring mowing client signed
```

---

### On-Site Talking Points (say this while finishing the job)

> *"Your yard looks great now — the last thing you want is for the grass to get out of control and undo all this work. We do weekly mowing starting at $120/mo — want us to just keep it up for you? We're already in the neighborhood."*

Variations:
- *"A lot of our cleanup customers add weekly mowing so they don't have to worry about it. First cut is on us if you sign up today."*
- *"We can be back next week for your first cut — lock in the price now before the summer route fills up."*

**💡 Key principle:** Don't pitch it as a separate sale. Frame it as protecting the investment they just made.

---

### Follow-Up Text Scripts

**Day 3 (after job):**
> *"Hey [Name], this is [Your name] from Lawn Masters V5. Hope you're loving how the yard looks! Just wanted to let you know our weekly mowing routes for your area are filling up for the summer. Want to lock in a spot? Starting at $90/mo biweekly or $120/mo weekly. Just reply and I'll get you set up."*

**Day 10 (if no reply):**
> *"Hey [Name] — last check-in from us! Summer mowing slots in [neighborhood] are almost full. Reply YES and we'll add you to the route, no paperwork needed."*

**Day 30 (cold re-engage):**
> *"Hey [Name], Lawn Masters V5 here. We're back in your area this week — want a quick cleanup or another pressure wash before the summer heat peaks? Also still have 1–2 mowing spots open in your neighborhood."*

### 🎬 Reel Concepts

- **PW time-lapse** — Wide angle, one driveway start to finish. 30 seconds. No music needed — the pressure washer sound IS the content.
- **The combo reveal** — Show the full property before, then fast-cut to after (PW + beds + cut lawn). 45–60 seconds. Text overlay: *"One call. Full property."*
